How it works

Precision through advanced optics & mathematics.

The exact process of extracting heart rate from light, start to finish.

01
Flash & Lens Guidance
Rest your finger over the camera. Optional Flash toggle for low-ambient environments, with real-time 54–135 BPM scanning range guidance.
02
Optical Sampling
The camera records at 30 frames per second to capture sub-dermal capillary light transmission variations.
03
NLMS Motion Cancellation
3-axis accelerometer adaptive filtering cancels hand motion and contact pressure shifts.
04
Multi-Domain Fusion
Combines FFT spectral decomposition with Autocorrelation (ACF) lag peaks for sub-harmonic resilience.
05
Quality Consensus
Learned feature weighting ($Q_i$) & cross-estimator agreement boost ensure clinical-grade precision.
Under the hood

Privacy & System Design

The entire algorithm runs locally on your device with native auto-updates.

100% Offline Capability
Operates seamlessly without active cellular data or Wi-Fi, keeping all signal extraction on-device.
Transient Processing
Video frames process in RAM in real time and are immediately discarded. No video is recorded or stored.
Flash & Light Control
OFF by default for battery efficiency; toggle ON for low-light conditions to boost signal SNR.
In-App Auto Update
Auto update available — one tap to use the latest algorithm engine.
Accuracy & Reliability

Clinical-Grade Validation

Mathematically benchmarked and verified across 158 subjects against gold-standard ECG/PPG sensors.

0.76 BPM MAE (Smartphone Video)
High-precision smartphone benchmark evaluated on 21 real finger-camera subjects (Welltory PPG dataset; Pearson r = 0.9895, 81.0% sub-BPM precision against the gold-standard Polar H10 chest-strap ECG reference).
0.61 BPM MAE (PhysioNet BIDMC)
Clinical benchmark evaluated across all 53 human subjects in PhysioNet BIDMC (0.61 BPM MAE clean subgroup / 3.84 BPM MAE full cohort including ICU motion signals; Pearson r = 0.9951).
Triple Consensus
Green, red, and PCA components analyzed independently to filter noise.
Adaptive Respiration
Filtering suppresses sub-harmonic respiratory sinus arrhythmia.
Statistical Trimming
Top & bottom 20% of sliding snapshots automatically trimmed.
